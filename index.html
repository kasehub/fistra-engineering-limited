About Fistra Engineering

Fistra Engineering Limited is a leading engineering firm specializing in structural and fire engineering. With close relations to research and development, we bring innovative solutions to complex engineering challenges.

Our team of experienced engineers are experts in their fields with extensive experience in both local and international projects. We combine technical expertise with practical knowledge to deliver safe, efficient, and sustainable engineering solutions.

Founded on principles of excellence and innovation, Fistra Engineering has built a reputation for delivering high-quality engineering services across various sectors including commercial, residential, industrial, and infrastructure projects.
