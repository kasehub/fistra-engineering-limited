About FiSTra Engineering

FiSTra Engineering is a specialist consultancy in structural and fire engineering, delivering expert solutions backed by extensive local and international experience. We are committed to providing cost-effective, practical designs tailored to meet each client's unique needs.

We serve diverse sectors such as mining, transportation, commercial, industrial, and residential—bringing precision, innovation, and reliability to every project.

Our name "FiSTra" represents our core values: Forward Thinking, Integrity, Safety, Technical Excellence, Reliability, and Adaptability.
