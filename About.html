Our Story

Fistra Engineering Limited was founded in 2005 with a vision to revolutionize the engineering industry through innovative solutions and unwavering commitment to excellence. What started as a small consultancy has grown into a recognized leader in structural and fire engineering.

Our name "Fistra" derives from the Latin word for "arch," symbolizing our commitment to creating strong, stable, and elegant solutions that stand the test of time. Just as an arch distributes weight efficiently, we aim to distribute knowledge and expertise throughout the industry.

Today, we are a team of over 50 dedicated professionals with expertise spanning structural engineering, fire safety, forensic analysis, and research development. Our engineers hold advanced degrees and certifications, with many recognized as leaders in their respective fields.
