Our Story

FiSTra Engineering Limited was founded in 2005 with a vision to revolutionize the engineering industry through innovative solutions and unwavering commitment to excellence. What started as a small consultancy has grown into a recognized leader in structural and fire engineering.

Our name "FiSTra" derives from the Latin word for "arch," symbolizing our commitment to creating strong, stable, and elegant solutions that stand the test of time. Just as an arch distributes weight efficiently, we aim to distribute knowledge and expertise throughout the industry.

Today, we are a team of dedicated professionals with expertise spanning structural engineering, fire safety, forensic analysis, and research development. Our engineers hold advanced degrees and certifications, with many recognized as leaders in their respective fields.

Leadership Team

Meet the experts behind FiSTra Engineering's success

Eng. Ernest Kakoma

Managing Director

A Zambian Fire and Structural Engineer with expertise in designing, analysing, and supervising complex infrastructure across Africa and Europe. He is a Professional Engineer (EIZ), Council Member of the Institution of Structural Engineers (UK & Southern Africa), and a BIM Panellist for IStructE.

He holds a Master of Engineering in Structural Engineering and Bachelor of Engineering in Civil and Environmental Engineering from The University of Zambia. He is currently pursuing a PhD in Civil Engineering at Stellenbosch University, his research focuses on fire structural engineering and evacuation modelling in train stations.
