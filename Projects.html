Sky Tower Complex

2023
New York, USA
Structural Engineering
Commercial

The Sky Tower Complex is a 40-story commercial building in the heart of New York's financial district. Fistra Engineering was responsible for the complete structural design and analysis of this innovative high-rise.

Our team implemented advanced seismic resistance features, including base isolation technology and damping systems, to ensure the building's resilience against extreme weather events. The design also incorporated sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems.

Key Features

  • Base isolation system for earthquake resistance
  • Wind tunnel testing for aerodynamic optimization
  • Use of high-performance concrete and steel composites
  • Green building certification targeting LEED Platinum
  • Integrated photovoltaic facade system

Results

The project was completed on schedule and within budget, receiving multiple industry awards for innovation in structural engineering and sustainable design. The building now stands as a landmark of modern engineering excellence.
