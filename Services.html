Structural Engineering

Our structural engineering services encompass the complete lifecycle of structures, from conceptual design to construction oversight and maintenance planning. We combine technical expertise with practical experience to deliver safe, efficient, and innovative structural solutions.

We specialize in both traditional and complex structures, utilizing the latest software and analysis techniques to ensure structural integrity and optimal performance.

  • Structural analysis and design
  • Seismic and wind engineering
  • Foundation design and analysis
  • Structural rehabilitation and retrofitting
  • Construction documentation and oversight
  • Expert witness services

Fire Engineering

Our fire engineering services focus on protecting people, property, and the environment from the devastating effects of fire. We employ performance-based design approaches that go beyond prescriptive code requirements to create innovative and effective fire safety solutions.

With expertise in fire dynamics, human behavior, and building systems, we develop comprehensive fire safety strategies tailored to each project's unique requirements.

  • Fire risk assessment and analysis
  • Evacuation planning and modeling
  • Fire resistance design
  • Smoke management system design
  • Fire detection and suppression system design
  • Performance-based design approaches
